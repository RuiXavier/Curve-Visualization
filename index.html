Controls

Click and Drag to draw a curve

Spacebar - Toggle animation

"C" - Clear all curves

"Z" - End a curve

"+" - Increase the number of segments

"-" - Decrease the number of segments

">" - Increase speed

"<" - Decrease speed

"P" - Toggle visibility of points

"L" - Toggle visibility of segments

"S" - Toggle special mode

Sliders

Speed: -100% 100%
Segments: 1 50
